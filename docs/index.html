Graduate Research Core Competencies Certificate

Practical skills for every stage of your research journey — citation management, scholarly communication, data management, literature review, publishing, and more.

Complete all modules at your own pace to earn your certificate.

What You’ll Learn

The Graduate Research Core Competencies Certificate is designed to support graduate students at all stages of their research journey by building practical, transferable skills essential to scholarly work.

Participants develop strategies for:

  • Effective citation management using tools like Zotero and Paperpile
  • Advanced literature review techniques including systematic and scoping reviews
  • Responsible research data management and open data publishing
  • Scholarly communication — ORCID, researcher identity, and open access
  • Publishing & sharing theses, dissertations, and research articles
  • Graduation requirements — formatting, ProQuest, and Graduate Division workflows

The modules can be completed in any order, at your own pace. A certificate will be awarded upon completion of all modules.

Submit Assignments on Canvas to Earn Points

The knowledge checks on this website mirror the quizzes on Canvas and are for self-study practice. To receive credit toward your certificate, complete and submit all assignments through the Canvas course platform.
